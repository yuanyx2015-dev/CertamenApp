# Privacy Policy for CertamenPrep

Last updated: March 28, 2026

## Information We Collect

CertamenPrep collects the following information:
- Email address and name (via Google or Apple Sign-In)
- Quiz performance and progress statistics
- Practice session history

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- Authenticate your account
- Save and display your quiz progress and statistics
- Improve the app experience

## Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase. We do not sell your data to third parties.

## Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile section of the app.
